Lil' Pilchuck Creek Farm

Kolton & Karen Bloom
LilPilchuckCreekFarm@gmail.com

Our hobby farm is home to ADGA Registered Nigerian Dwarfs. The farm has evolved from a farm girl's wish to continue her dairy roots into a 4-H project with the goal of raising show-quality goats. Thanks to wonderful breed mentors we have established a solid herd we hope to learn from and build on.
